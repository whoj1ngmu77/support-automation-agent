COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT - ABC Technologies

REFUND POLICY
Customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase for annual subscriptions.
Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable after 7 days of the billing cycle.
All refund requests above $500 require supervisor approval before processing.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION POLICY
Customers may cancel their subscription at any time from the account settings page.
Cancellation requests must be reviewed by a human supervisor to confirm no active
contractual obligations exist before final approval.

ACCOUNT CLOSURE POLICY
Account closure requests require identity verification and supervisor sign-off.
Customer data is retained for 90 days post-closure, then permanently deleted.

COMPENSATION POLICY
Compensation requests exceeding $100 require supervisor approval. Compensation
under $100 may be auto-approved by support agents.
