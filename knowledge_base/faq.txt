FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS - ABC Technologies

Q: How do I reset my account password?
A: Go to the login page, click "Forgot Password", follow the emailed reset link.
Links expire after 30 minutes.

Q: How do I update my profile information?
A: Account Settings > Profile, edit details, click Save.

Q: How do I activate or deactivate my account?
A: Deactivation is under Account Settings > Account Status. Reversible within 90 days.

Q: How do I change my subscription plan?
A: Billing > Subscription > Change Plan. Upgrades apply immediately with prorated billing.
